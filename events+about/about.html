Womens Forum

IIT Ropar

Womens Forum, IIT Ropar is established to create an environment which boosts all-round development of women members in campus. It also includes WISE(Women in Science & Engineering).


    Aim:-

  • To create a platform where women can raise their voice and be on par with men.
  • To work towards a gender sensitive and safe environment.
  • To broaden the domain of knowledge and improve skills of women community.

    Activities planned by Women's Forum:-

  • Conduct workshops for students/staff to increase technical skills.
  • Organise talks by eminent personalities to motivate campus community.
  • Conduct cultural and technical competitions on regular basis.
  • Organise trips to understand real life society.
  • Celebrating International Women's Day.
