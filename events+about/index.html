Technical talk by Minal Rohit

Date:March 8,2017

Technical talk is to be given by Minal Rohit on March 8th,2017.Minal Rohit is one of the women scientists who helped India to reach Mars in very first attempt. She is a spunky 38 year old project manager on MOM(Mission on Mars). She lead a team of 500 scientists as a systems engineer at ISRO. She aims to become the first women director to head a national space agency.
